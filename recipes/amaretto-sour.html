Amaretto Sour

Description

A sweet and sour cocktail thats not too strong.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 oz - Amaretto
  • 3/4 oz - Bourbon
  • 1 oz - Fresh Squeezed Lime Juice
  • 1/4 oz - Simple Syrup
  • 1/2 oz - Egg White

Steps

  1. Fill shaker cup with ice
  2. Add all ingredients to shaker
  3. Shake vigorously for 30 seconds
  4. Strain into a rocks glass over fresh ice
  5. Optional: Add a drop or two of bitters on top
