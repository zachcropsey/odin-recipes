Classic Aviation

Description

Classic Aviation cocktail. An old favorite with simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz - Gin
  • 1/2 oz - Fresh Squeezed Lime Juice
  • 1/2 oz - Maraschino Liqueur
  • 1/4 oz - Creme de Violette

Steps

  1. Fill shaker cup with ice
  2. Add all ingredients to shaker
  3. Shake vigorously for 30 seconds
  4. Strain into a cocktail glass and enjoy
