Smugglers Cove Daiquiri

Description

The famous Smugglers Cove Daiquiri No.1. A very simple yet delicious cocktail.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz - Probitas Rum
  • 3/4 oz - Fresh Squeezed Lime Juice
  • 1/2 oz - Demerara Syrup

Steps

  1. Fill shaker cup with ice
  2. Add all ingredients to shaker
  3. Shake vigorously for 30 seconds
  4. Strain into an up glass and enjoy
